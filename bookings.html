Venue Bookings

To ensure clarity and trust among all teams, the following rules apply to the booking of match venues. Transparency here allows all teams to plan their travel and schedules with confidence.

Booking Rules

📸
Proof of Booking
The team responsible for booking a venue must provide proof of the booking to all teams in their allocated group. Acceptable formats: screenshot, email confirmation, or official venue receipt.
📍
Required Information
The proof of booking must clearly display the following: Venue Name & Address, Date of the Match, Start and End Time, and Total Amount Paid (if shown on receipt).
✍️
Manager Responsibility
If the venue does not provide receipts or confirmations with all required details, the booking manager must share a simple signed document confirming the arrangement.

Manager Signed Document

When a venue receipt is unavailable or incomplete, the signed document from the booking manager must confirm all of the following:

📍 Venue: Full name and address of the facility being booked.
📅 Date: The exact date on which the match is scheduled to take place.
⏰ Time: The confirmed start and end time of the booking.
💰 Payment: A statement confirming that the necessary fees have been arranged and are covered.
✍️ Responsibility: The manager takes full personal responsibility for the accuracy of all the above information.
Purpose: This process promotes transparency and allows all teams to plan their travel and schedules with confidence, fostering trust and cooperation within the brotherhood.
Travel Guidance: For parking and directions, it is best to ask the brother who booked the venue directly. All vehicles must be parked in designated areas only — do not block emergency exits or access roads.
