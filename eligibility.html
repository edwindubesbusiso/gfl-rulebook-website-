Requirements for Applicants

Player Requirements

🤝
Community Affiliation
All players must have a verifiable connection to the community — known to the organizers or formally invited by a current member in good standing.
📋
Code of Conduct
Applicants must read and acknowledge the GFL Rule Book and agree to uphold the league's values of respect, fair play, and integrity before playing.
📢
Bible Study Players
If affiliated players are Bible studies, organizers must be made aware of the number of non-publishers and give their go-ahead before they play.
Manager Duty: It is the responsibility of a team's Manager to inform the opposing team's Manager of the number and standing of any non-publisher players before the match takes place.
Team Rights: Teams hold the right to reject playing a match if they have not been properly made aware of players who are not Jehovah's Witnesses.
ZERO TOLERANCE — Cornerstone Rule: This affiliation rule is a cornerstone of the league. Any team found to be dishonest on this account will be eliminated from the league with immediate effect. No questions asked.

Age Limit

16+
Standard Entry
All players must be at least 16 years of age to participate in the GFL. Proof of age may be requested at any time by the organizers.
<16
Junior Players
Players aged below 16 must have a signed parental or guardian consent form, issued and filed with the league organizers, before they are permitted to play.
Note: The consent form for junior players is issued by the league organizers and must be on file prior to any match participation, in case of an incident during play.
