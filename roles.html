Community
Roles & Responsibilities

The GFL is run by a community of dedicated individuals. Each role carries specific responsibilities that together ensure the league operates smoothly, fairly, and in the spirit of fellowship.

👨‍💼
Manager

The Manager is the leader of the team and a role model for players and spectators alike.

  • Register all players and organize team sheets for every match
  • Ensure the team is ready to play and informed of all updates
  • Uphold the GFL Code of Conduct at all times on and off the pitch
  • Manage player behaviour and substitutions to maintain the spirit of the game
  • Act as the primary point of contact between the team and league organizers
  • Inform the opposing Manager of the number and standing of any non-publisher players
  • Stay alert to any changes and information shared in the group
  • Help improve the JW League wherever possible
🗂️
Organisers

The league organizers are responsible for the overall administration and smooth running of the GFL.

  • Create the season fixture lists
  • Address and resolve disputes that may arise between teams
  • Disseminate all league information, updates, and reminders to all teams
  • Ensure the league rules are applied fairly across all teams
  • Manage registration and all administrative tasks required for the league to function
🛠️
Support Team

The support team consists of brothers who work behind the scenes to ensure the league runs smoothly.

  • Manage the league website and communication channels
  • Handle the Top 8 rankings and related content
  • Plan for future expansion and improvement of the league
  • Provide back-end operational support to the organizers
⚖️
Disciplinary Team

The disciplinary team is an independent body that reviews incidents and ensures accountability across the entire league.

  • Review all red card incidents and serious reports from match reports
  • Impose appropriate sanctions such as match bans or fines
  • Hear formal appeals from players or teams regarding disciplinary decisions
  • Ensure disciplinary measures are applied fairly and consistently for all teams
Appeals Process: The Disciplinary Team's appeals process is subject to a defined timeline. Players and teams wishing to appeal a decision must do so within the set period. The Disciplinary Team's ruling on appeal is final.
