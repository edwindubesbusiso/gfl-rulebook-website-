Gaming Structure
Points & Standings

Allocation of Points

The league table is determined using the standard three-point system to encourage attacking and entertaining football.

🏆  Win 3 PTS
🤝  Draw 1 PT
❌  Loss 0 PTS

Tie-Breaker Order

If two or more teams are level on points at the end of the season, the following criteria are applied in this exact order to determine final positions:

  • 1
    Goal Difference
    Total Goals Scored minus Total Goals Conceded across all league matches.
  • 2
    Goals Scored
    Total number of goals scored throughout the season. More goals scored ranks higher.
  • 3
    Head-to-Head Record
    The result(s) of the match(es) played directly between the tied teams during the season.
  • 4
    Fewest Red Cards
    The team with the lowest number of red cards received across the season ranks higher.
  • 5
    Fewest Yellow Cards
    The team with the lowest number of yellow cards received across the season ranks higher.
  • 6
    Coin Toss
    As a last resort, a coin toss conducted by the League Organizer will determine the final position.
Note: All tie-breaker criteria are applied strictly in the order listed above. Only if criterion 1 cannot separate teams will criterion 2 be applied, and so on.
